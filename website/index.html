Welcome to Cedar Garden

A family-owned Lebanese restaurant serving authentic flavors since day one

Cedar Garden is a family-owned Lebanese restaurant serving classic mezze, charcoal-grilled kebabs, shawarma, and traditional desserts in a warm, casual setting.

We pride ourselves on using fresh ingredients, traditional recipes passed down through generations, and halal-certified meats. Every dish is prepared with care and served with the warmth of Lebanese hospitality.
